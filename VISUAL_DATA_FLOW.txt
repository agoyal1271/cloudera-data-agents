================================================================================
KEY INSIGHT: CMM-Style Hierarchy in Action
================================================================================

The example shows how a single "problem" (high RRC rejection rate at Cell 123)
ripples through the system:

  Raw Counter (9,000 requests)
    ↓
  Cell-Level KPI (93.33% success rate ⚠️ WARNING)
    ↓
  Root Cause Analysis (3.56% capacity-based rejections ❌ CRITICAL)
    ↓
  Drill-Down Hierarchy (Carrier → Spectrum → Area to find extent)
    ↓
  Business Impact ($2,400/day revenue loss)
    ↓
  Autonomous Agent Detection (SLA_Monitor, Anomaly_Detector, Capacity_Planner)
    ↓
  Human Decision & Action (Load balancing + hardware procurement)
    ↓
  Resolution & Cost Recovery (SLA restored, revenue protected)

This is exactly how CMM-23.5's hierarchical metadata design enables
operational excellence: from raw counters to business outcomes.
